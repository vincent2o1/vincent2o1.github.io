BrainX

A brain-inspired AI project connecting perception, memory, emotion, damage response, imagination, and Unity simulation.

Architecture Unity Simulation Emotion Demos

Architecture

BrainX uses multiple modules such as visual input, hearing input, ring-tree memory, emotional centers, reflex memory, imagination, and Unity-based motor control.

Unity Simulation

The Unity agent receives commands from Python, moves inside a simulation, detects damage, and sends sensor information back to the AI system.

Emotional Centers

The emotional system detects negative states, stores recovery patterns, and forms reflex-like responses after damage recovery.
