Architecture

Perception

BrainX receives visual and hearing inputs, processes them through specialized modules, and converts them into internal representations.

Memory

The ring-tree memory system stores patterns, compares new inputs, and retrieves related past experiences.

Emotion and Reflex

Emotional centers detect negative states, store recovery patterns, and create reflex-like responses after damage recovery.

Unity Simulation

The Unity agent receives commands from Python, moves inside a simulation, detects damage through sensor values, and sends feedback back to BrainX.
