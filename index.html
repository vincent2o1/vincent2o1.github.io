BrainX - CrX arch

Summary

BrainX is a brain-inspired AI project that connects perception, memory, emotion, damage response, imagination, and Unity simulation.

Unity Simulation

The Unity agent receives commands from Python, moves inside a simulation, detects damage, and sends feedback back to BrainX.

Demo Videos

Demo videos and screenshots will be added here.

Architecture

Perception

BrainX receives visual and hearing inputs and converts them into internal representations.

Memory

The ring-tree memory system stores patterns, compares new inputs, and retrieves related past experiences.

Emotion and Reflex

Emotional centers detect negative states, store recovery patterns, and create reflex-like responses.

Theory

Brain-Inspired Intelligence

BrainX is based on the idea that intelligence can emerge from the interaction of perception, memory, emotional signals, and action.

Emotion as Control Signal

Negative and positive states are treated as signals that help the system learn danger, recovery, and useful behavior patterns.

Reflex Learning

When the system experiences damage and later recovers, it stores the recovery sequence as a reflex-like response for future situations.
